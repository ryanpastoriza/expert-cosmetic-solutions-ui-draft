Treatment Overview

Advanced Laser Skin Rejuvenation

Laser skin rejuvenation at Expert Cosmetic Solutions uses Candela technology to stimulate collagen, improve texture and address fine lines, enlarged pores, uneven tone, congestion and acne scarring. Treatment is non-ablative, so most clients return to normal activities the same day with only mild redness.

A course of four to six sessions, four weeks apart, is usually recommended. Your nurse will confirm suitability and timing at your free consultation.

Care Instructions

Preparation & Aftercare

Pre-Treatment Care

  • Stay out of the sun or solariums for at least two weeks before your treatment. Do not use any form of tanning throughout your treatment, including accidental sun exposure during daily activities.
  • Sun exposure before and after laser treatments will increase the risk of complications or side effects, such as pigmentation and burns.
  • Do not use self-tanning lotions or sprays for two weeks before your treatment. The procedure may have to be postponed if you have tan on.
  • Do not wax, pluck or bleach for two weeks before and after your treatments, as this will increase the risk of complications.
  • Do not use Retin A for two weeks prior to treatment.
  • Do not use contraindicated drugs for six months prior to treatment.

Aftercare

  • A mild to moderate redness may occur and should fade within a few hours after treatment.
  • A good skincare regime is recommended to get the most out of your treatments, including a Hyaluronic Acid to help with hydration and a Vitamin A to help with resurfacing.
  • Wash face with a gentle cleanser and cool water 48 hours post treatment.
  • Do not use a scrub for up to seven days after treatment.
  • Do not wax the area for five days.
  • Apply moisturiser and sunscreen (SPF 50+) every day after 3 days post treatment.
  • Avoid sun for two weeks post treatment.
  • Avoid activities where you are likely to perspire heavily for 24 to 48 hours.
